Privacy Policy — JumpTrack

Last updated: May 7, 2026

JumpTrack ("the App") is a fitness application developed by Sakovkid. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or contact information.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.
  • We do not send your workout data, camera images, or videos to our servers.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.

Camera Permission

The App requests access to your device camera so it can detect body movement and count jumps during a workout. Camera frames are processed on your device only. The App does not upload camera frames, photos, or videos to any server.

Workout Data Stored Locally

The App may store workout history locally on your device, including jump count, workout duration, pace, progress, records, and achievements. This information is stored only on your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Data Retention and Deletion

Workout history is kept locally on your device until you delete it. You can delete workout data inside the App by opening Profile and tapping "Delete workout data." You can also remove all local App data by uninstalling JumpTrack or by using your device's system settings to clear the App's storage. Because JumpTrack does not upload workout data to our servers, there is no server-side account or cloud database for us to delete.

Sharing Results

If you choose to share a workout result, the App uses Android's standard sharing options. You decide which app or service receives the shared result. Any information you share through another app is handled according to that app's own privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
