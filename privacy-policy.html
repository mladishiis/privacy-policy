Privacy Policy — ProfiEnglish

Last updated: April 4, 2026

ProfiEnglish ("the App") is a free English vocabulary learning application developed by Sakovkid. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.

Data Stored Locally

All learning progress (words studied, correct answers, mistakes) is stored exclusively on your device using a local database. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Text-to-Speech

The App uses Android's built-in Text-to-Speech engine to pronounce English words. This feature uses the TTS engine installed on your device and does not send data to our servers.

App Ratings

If you choose to rate the App, you will be redirected to the Google Play Store. Any rating or review is governed by Google Play's own privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
